About PiiTrade

We believe every retail trader deserves the same data-driven edge that institutional desks have had for decades. PiiTrade was built to close that gap — for free.

It provides real-time buy, sell, and hold signals for 35 forex pairs — including majors, minors, and exotics — as well as commodities (gold, silver, crude oil), major cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, XRP, SOL, BNB), and top US equities (AAPL, TSLA, NVDA, MSFT, AMZN, GOOGL, META).

No subscription, no credit card, and no account are required to access the signal dashboard. Our entire platform runs in your browser. Every signal, every chart, every tool is free.

🎯 Our Mission

The forex market trades over $7.5 trillion per day, yet the sophisticated analytical tools used by hedge funds and proprietary trading firms are typically locked behind expensive subscriptions, Bloomberg terminals, or in-house quant teams inaccessible to most retail traders.

PiiTrade's mission is simple: democratise access to AI-powered market analysis. We built an automated machine-learning pipeline that processes 10+ years of historical price data, real-time exchange rates, news sentiment, and macroeconomic indicators — and we give the resulting signals away for free, to everyone, without restriction.

We believe that with the right tools, retail traders can make more informed decisions, manage risk more effectively, and participate in financial markets on a more level playing field.

🛠️ What PiiTrade Offers

  • Real-time AI trading signals: Our advanced AI model generates buy, sell, and hold signals with confidence scores for each supported pair. Signals update continuously as new price data arrives.
  • Live exchange rate dashboard: View current bid/ask prices for all 35 forex majors, minors, and exotic pairs sourced from live institutional data feeds.
  • Volatility scanner: Identify which pairs are moving the most across 1-hour, 4-hour, and 24-hour timeframes — ideal for breakout traders and scalpers.
  • Smart money concepts (SMC) scanner: Automated detection of Fair Value Gaps (FVG), Break of Structure (BOS), Change of Character (CHoCH), and order blocks across multiple timeframes.
  • Support & resistance breakout scanner: Real-time alerts when price approaches or breaks key horizontal levels on any tracked pair.
  • Position-size risk calculator: Input your account balance, risk percentage, stop-loss pips, and pair — instantly calculate your optimal lot size, pip value, and potential gain/loss in USD.
  • Market alerts & economic event feed: Live feed of Federal Reserve decisions, FOMC statements, significant price surges, and institutional market activity.
  • Signal accuracy history: 30-day rolling win/loss chart per pair so you can objectively evaluate how the model has performed on each asset.
  • Educational resources: Our methodology page, blog, and risk disclaimer help traders understand how signals are generated and how to use them safely.

🤖 Our Technology

PiiTrade's signal engine is built on a proprietary machine learning model — the same class of models used by professional quantitative trading firms. The model is trained on:

  • Years of daily OHLCV (Open/High/Low/Close/Volume) price data
  • Technical indicators: RSI, MACD, EMA, Bollinger Bands, and more
  • Momentum features: daily returns, short-term and medium-term rolling momentum
  • News sentiment: aggregated from financial headlines and market news
  • Macroeconomic indicators: inflation data, PMI readings, central bank interest-rate differentials

The model undergoes rigorous cross-validation to prevent data leakage and is retrained regularly. Real-time predictions are served via our backend that fetches live prices from institutional-grade data sources.

👤 Who Is PiiTrade For?

PiiTrade is designed for a wide range of traders and market observers:

  • Retail forex traders who want a data-driven second opinion before entering a trade, without paying hundreds of dollars a month for signal services.
  • Beginners learning to trade who want to see how AI analyses market conditions and understand concepts like support/resistance, RSI divergence, and momentum.
  • Experienced traders using PiiTrade as a confluence tool — combining the AI signal with their own technical analysis and risk management.
  • Crypto enthusiasts tracking BTC, ETH, XRP, SOL, and BNB signals alongside their favourite forex pairs.
  • Students and researchers interested in algorithmic trading and how machine learning is applied to financial markets.

📅 PiiTrade Timeline

2024 – Early Development
Initial concept: build a free, AI-driven alternative to expensive signal services. First prototype trained on EUR/USD data using gradient-boosted trees.
Late 2024 – Expansion
Coverage expanded to 35 forex pairs. AI model stabilised with time-series cross-validation. Beta launched to early users.
Early 2025 – Multi-Asset Launch
Commodities (gold, silver, oil), major cryptocurrencies, and top US stocks added to the platform. Advanced SMC analysis tools introduced.
2025 – Dashboard & Tools
Live signal dashboard launched publicly. Volatility scanner, risk calculator, support/resistance scanner, and 30-day accuracy history rolled out.
2026 – Ongoing
Continuous model retraining, accuracy improvements, and new feature development. Upcoming: multi-timeframe confluence scoring, TradingView integration, mobile push alerts, and API access for developers.
