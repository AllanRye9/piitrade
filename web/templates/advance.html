📖 How to Read This Page (click to expand — great for beginners!)
📍
Current Price
The glowing horizontal line on the chart. It shows exactly where the market is right now for the selected pair. The price badge updates automatically.
⚡
ENTRY
The price level where the AI recommends opening a trade. Look for this dashed line on the chart and the bold value in the signal card below.
✅
Take Profit (TP)
The target price where you close the trade to lock in profit. Shown in green. Always further from Entry than the Stop Loss for a positive risk:reward.
🛑
Stop Loss (SL)
The price level at which you exit if the trade moves against you — to limit losses. Shown in red. Never trade without a Stop Loss!
📦
Order Blocks (OB)
Price zones where large institutions (banks, hedge funds) placed big orders. Price often returns to these zones. Green = bullish OB (buy zone); Red = bearish OB (sell zone).
🌀
Fair Value Gap (FVG)
A gap in the market where price moved so fast no trades happened. Price tends to revisit these gaps. Blue = bullish FVG; Orange = bearish FVG.
💧
Liquidity Zones
Areas where many stop-loss orders are clustered (usually above recent highs or below recent lows). Institutions target these areas. Watch the S/R Levels section for these zones.
🔀
BOS / CHoCH
Break of Structure (BOS): price confirms the current trend by breaking past a key level. Change of Character (CHoCH): signals a potential trend reversal — pay attention!
