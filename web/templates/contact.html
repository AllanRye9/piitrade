❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is PiiTrade really free?

Yes — completely free, forever. You can access all 35 forex pair signals, the volatility scanner, risk calculator, SMC scanner, and all other tools without creating an account or providing payment details.

How accurate are the trading signals?

Signal accuracy varies by pair and market conditions. You can view the 30-day rolling accuracy history for each pair directly on the Dashboard. Past accuracy does not guarantee future results — please read our Risk Disclaimer.

How often are signals updated?

Signals update dynamically as new price data arrives. The model checks for updated rates and recalculates indicators on each page load. The underlying AI model is retrained regularly on fresh market data.

Do I need to create an account to use PiiTrade?

No — the dashboard is fully accessible without an account. You only need to register if you want to save preferences or receive email signal alerts.

I found a bug — how do I report it?

Please email bugs@piitrade.com with a description of the issue, the page URL, and your browser/OS. Screenshots are very helpful. We aim to respond within 1 business day.

Can I use PiiTrade signals for automated trading?

Our signals are intended for informational and educational purposes. We do not provide direct broker integration or automated order execution. Always conduct your own research and consult a licensed financial adviser before trading.
