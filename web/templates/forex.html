🧠 How Our AI Works

Our AI analyzes 10+ years of historical price data, real-time news sentiment, and economic events using machine learning to generate buy/sell/hold signals. The model is retrained weekly using fresh market data collected automatically via Zapier from Yahoo Finance.

The pipeline uses a LightGBM gradient-boosted tree model trained on features including RSI, MACD, exponential moving averages (EMA-20/50), news sentiment scores, CPI delta, and PMI readings. Training data is stored in Google Sheets and predictions are displayed here in real time.
