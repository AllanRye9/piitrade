🧠 AI Suggestions: The app learns from your voice usage and surfaces your most-used commands as quick-tap chips in the voice bar. Command data is stored locally in SQLite – never sent to third parties.


🖼️ Landscape / Portrait: Click the rectangle icon in the toolbar to toggle between 16:9 widescreen and 4:3 portrait aspect ratios.


🎨 Themes: Click the palette icon in the toolbar to choose from Dark, Light, Ocean, Forest, or Sunset colour themes.


🔊 Read Aloud: Click the speaker icon to open the Text-to-Speech panel. Choose a voice / accent, adjust speed, select a language, then click Read Slide to have the slide content read aloud using your browser's built-in speech synthesis engine.


🤖 AI Analysis: Click the brain icon to run an AI analysis of the current slide. The engine extracts keywords, generates an extractive summary, estimates reading time, and classifies the slide's sentiment – all locally, with no data leaving your browser session.


📊 Chart Analysis: When viewing an image slide (chart), click 📊 Analyze Chart in the AI panel to detect dominant colors, brightness, colorfulness, and receive a best-effort chart-type hint (bar, pie, line, or scatter). CSV files can also be uploaded directly and will be displayed as searchable tables.


ℹ️ Voice recognition uses the browser's built-in Web Speech API (powered by Google's free speech AI in Chrome/Edge). Works best in Chrome or Edge with microphone permission granted.
